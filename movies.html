DUNE

In Theaters and HBO Max Denis Villeneuve ("Arrival," "Blade Runner 2049") directs "Dune," a big-screen adaptation of Frank Herbert's landmark novel. "Dune" is a mythic and emotionally packed hero's journey that follows Paul Atreides, a smart and gifted young man born into a tremendous destiny beyond his understanding, who must travel to the most perilous planet in the universe to protect the future of his family and people. As terrible forces clash over the planet's sole supply of the most valuable resource in existence—a commodity capable of unleashing humanity's full potential—only those who can overcome their fear will live. The film stars Oscar nominee Timothée Chalamet ("Call Me By")...
